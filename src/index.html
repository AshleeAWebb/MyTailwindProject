MAKE YOUR IMPACT WITH LEADERSHIP SOCIETY

The contributions of volunteers and donors are invaluable, and their support is the cornerstone of our ability to serve the community. A heartfelt thank you goes out to everyone who has played a part in sustaining our collective efforts. In recognition of this invaluable support, all members will be granted access to special benefits as a token of our deep appreciation.

NOVICE
DONORS


    All members and donors contributing $500 or more will enjoy the following perks:

  • Engaging and fulfilling volunteer engagements
  • Opportunities for leadership skill enhancement through professional growth activities
  • Exclusive invitations to special events
  • Networking prospects with leading business figures in the region

ARTISAN
DONORS


    Members who contribute between $1,500 or more will enjoy these benefits:

  • All perks available to the initial level of donors
  • Entry to select volunteer engagements
  • Opportunities for unique professional growth activities
  • Advancement prospects through participation in leadership roles

VIRTUOSO
DONORS


    Members who contribute $10,000 or more will receive the following benefits:

  • All perks available to previous donation tiers
  • Priority access to volunteer activities
  • Special pricing options for certain events
  • Exclusive invitations to donor recognition gatherings
  • Development prospects via high-level leadership roles
